ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Chris Sharp.

I grew up around computers in a time where the internet was just getting started and have been fortunate enough to grow as technology has become what it is today. I have always had an affinity to understand how the technology I use works even when it doesn't have to do with my job. It's certainly helped me with my career, but it also keeps me passionate about what I do. Everything you see below highlights the things I have done in my various roles over my almost two decades in IT and shows I have worked hard to get to where I am at today.
